About

Hi! My name is Ahmed Naveed.

I am a full-stack web developer and this site is a portfolio for some of my web-development projects.

Other than that, I love table-tennis player and playing the guitar. I'm part of Dartmouth's table tennis club and I post new guitar instrumentals to my social media almost every week.
